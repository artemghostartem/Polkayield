Connecting the Dots

Cardano will allow the Paribus protocol to remain chain agnostic and simultaneously connect innumerable assets across multiple blockchains. We are interested in working with the most promising teams developing new ways to create and store value, regardless of where they decide to develop.

Cardano’s interoperability removes the restrictions imposed by a siloed ecosystem and allows value to flow freely, increasing opportunity for the protocol and it’s users.

The PBX Token

PBX is Paribus’s native governance token, allowing holders to create and vote on proposals relating to protocol guidance. The PBX token’s ultimate goal is to align the incentives across the Paribusyeild protocol, creating a codified harmony between stakeholders, the protocol itself, and the security of the assets contained within it.

PBX token holders are also entitled to a percentage of fees earned by the protocol, relative to their stake. The bigger the stake, the greater the tier, and the larger the percentage earned.
